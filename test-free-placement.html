üéÆ User Instructions

Placing Accessories:

  1. Select a pegboard from the list
  2. Click on an accessory you want to place
  3. Move your mouse over the pegboard - you'll see a preview
  4. Green preview = valid position, Red preview = invalid (overlap or out of bounds)
  5. Click on a green position to place the accessory
  6. Click again to place another one in a different spot
  7. Keep clicking to place as many as you want
  8. Press ESC when you're done placing that accessory type
